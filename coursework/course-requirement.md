# Course Requirement

All RPg students, including transfer-in PhD students, are required to complete the Coursework/Credit Requirements before thesis submission according to the following:

| Programme | Academic Integrity and Ethics / Ethics Subject# | Seminars | Practicum | Other Subjects | Total Credits | Education on “Understanding China and the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, P.R.C.” |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| MPhil | 1 credit | 2 credits | N/A | 6 credits with no more than 3 credits from [Guided-study subjects](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/gs/rpghandbook/section8-2-5) | credit bearing; 0 to 2 subjects (ranging from 0 to 5 credits) subject to RLSA result of a student | Non-credit bearing; complete an e-learning module and pass an assessment before thesis submission. |
| PhD* (3-year FT / 6-year PT) | 3 credits | 2 credits | 9 credits with no more than 6 credits from [Guided-study subjects](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/gs/rpghandbook/section8-2-5) | 15 credits |  | Non-credit bearing; complete an e-learning module and pass an assessment before thesis submission. |
| PhD* (4-year FT / 8-year PT) | 4 credits | 2 credits | 15 credits with no more than 9 credits from [Guided-study subjects](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/gs/rpghandbook/section8-2-5) | 22 credits |  | Non-credit bearing; complete an e-learning module and pass an assessment before thesis submission. |
