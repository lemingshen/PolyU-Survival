# TA

Every PhD student must serve as a Teaching Assistant (TA) throughout the PhD period. The TA experience is an integral part of your academic journey, providing you with valuable teaching experience and the opportunity to contribute to the academic community.

## Select Your Course

Visit the [TA Matching System Portal](https://tamatch.comp.polyu.edu.hk/) to select your preferred course for TA duties. The portal provides a list of available courses, along with their descriptions and requirements. Make sure to review the course details carefully before making your selection.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Make sure to read the email from time to time to check the notification of selecting the course. The email will be sent to your PolyU email account, so please check it regularly.
{% endhint %}

## TA Evaluation System

After completing your TA duties, you will be evaluated based on your performance. The evaluation process is designed to provide constructive feedback and help you improve your teaching skills. You can access the evaluation system through the [TA Evaluation Portal](https://tamatch.comp.polyu.edu.hk/tae/my_subject.php).
